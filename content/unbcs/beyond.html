Student Life at UNB


Computer science at UNB Fredericton offers a great student community.


Each student joins a house and competes throughout the year for the house cup. Houses gain points through many events such as dodgeball, pumpkin carving, charity drives, videogame marathons and more.


Also, join the Computer Science Association and participate in many social events during the year including movie nights, pancake breakfasts and our annual End-of-Year Celebration Banquet.
