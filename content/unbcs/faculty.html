Student Life in Computer Science at UNB

Encouraging Environment

UNB’s Computer Science program has lots of opportunities for students. Free tutoring is offered to first year students, and there is always a friendly face willing to help outside of tutoring hours.

Concurrent Degrees

There is plenty of room in the course schedule for specializations and concurrent degree programs, allowing students to work simultaneously towards a degree in Computer Science and a degree in Arts or Sciences.

Computer Science Association

Also known as the "CSA", the Computer Science Association hosts exciting events throughout the year, such as board game nights, bake sales, lock-ins and more. Becoming a member gives students discounts on many other computer science events such as pancake breakfast and the end of year banquet.

House Cup

During their first year students are placed in separate houses, named after famous influencial computer scientists. Once assigned a house students are a member for life. Throughout the year houses compete for the house cup, gaining points through participation and victory in events like dodgeball, pumpkin carving, charity drives, videogame marathons and more.
