Applying to Computer Science at UNB

If you find yourself interested in computer science, consider applying to become a student at UNB and join our faculty! You can apply any time you'd like; we're always looking for prospective students!

The best time to apply is before March 31st, as this ensures the best possible chance of getting in for the next academic school year. To get started, on a computer or mobile device, navigate to apply.unb.ca and start your application! We hope to see you soon!
