Requesting More Information

Still on the fence?

If you'd like to get more information about UNB and its faculties, including the Faculty of Computer Science, we can send you an information guide free of charge! To get the guide, on a computer or mobile device, navigate to unb.ca/admissions/request-information and fill out the required information.

If you'd like to come visit us, you can also book a tour of the campus and the Faculty of Computer Science! You'll get to ask us questions and even sit in on a class! To book a tour, on a computer or mobile device, navigate to unb.ca/admissions/book-a-campus-tour, pick a campus, and fill out the required information.
